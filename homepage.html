“STAND FOR JUSTICE AND FREEDOM IN PALESTINE!”

The struggle for Palestinian freedom is a call for justice that resonates across the world. Every day, millions stand in solidarity with the Palestinian people, advocating for their fundamental rights, dignity, and sovereignty.

Through peaceful demonstrations, education, and humanitarian efforts, we can amplify their voices and push for lasting change. Join us in supporting this movement for justice, freedom, and equality.


Solidarity Protest

📍 Location: Paris, France

📅 Date: 12th March 2024
